Our mission

Who are we?

In this hectic age we live in it's easy to lose touch with what it means to be a person that's part of a society. Studentsgift is an organisation that is dedicated to rekindeling this sense of belonging by bringing people together through giving. As students who gift we always look for oppertunities to be compassionate, to share and to love our fellow citizen. After all it's sharing what we have that truly makes us come to life!

"We make a living by what we get We make a life by what we give"

-Winston Churchhill
